To remove a line from the network

1. Click the line you want to
   delete on the map
2. In the Feature Table
   toolbar click on Manage Transit lines and system data.
3. The Transit Data Editor
   will open, and the selected line will be highlighted:

   ![](GUID-F1AA5006-0FF3-4DE4-A9D4-B43B2738921B-low.png)
4. Click on Remove Line to
   delete it.

![](GUID-8A73B75A-04F8-434D-A871-13EEB05FE0A0-low.png)

Alternatively:

1. Right-click on a map layer
   Open the Transit
   Editor.
2. Use the Filter.. bar to
   display specific line names and find the appropriate one more easily
3. Click on the line name to
   select it > Remove Line

Note: The action of
removing a line cannot be undone.
