To order data in an ascending or descending order of a
specific column/field:

- Right-click on a
  field/column>
   > Sort Ascending

  Note: A black arrow
  pointing up will appear to the right of sorted the field name .
  ![](GUID-6D41B8E6-203F-4062-BC48-BB3B67699D71-low.png)
- Right-click on a
  field/column > Sort Descending

  Note: A black arrow
  pointing down will appear to the right of the sorted field name.![](GUID-2B3E600C-B7A8-40F5-B0BD-D50814039486-low.png)
- To clear out any sorted data
  in the table:
  - Click on the Clear sort
    data in the toolbar
    ![](GUID-59212D42-60E0-48C7-8633-0A42C2480CF0-low.png)
  - Right-click on the
    sorted column > Clear
    Sort
