# Add/Update Transit System Information

In this tutorial, we will look at how to add and update the
transit system information such as modes, operators, and vehicle types.

[

**Your browser does not support HTML5 video.**

[Click here to play the video](GUID-42C081A6-200D-4CD9-B22E-519819F04385-low.mp4).

](GUID-42C081A6-200D-4CD9-B22E-519819F04385-low.mp4)

1. Select the Public Transport (PT) dataset in the map
table of contents.

![](GUID-BB527B9E-3A51-4BA4-8E13-7B657E1828F1-low.png)

2. Right-click the PT dataset > Open Transit System Data.

![](GUID-061D0A9D-F180-4B15-8A19-23D81D4BFA7A-low.png)

3. Within the Transit System Data window, click on the Modes
tab.

![](GUID-D9DE9745-1034-45B6-98D3-4414EA5A9427-low.png)

4. To add a new mode, click on Add Mode.

![](GUID-538675E8-B704-4B7E-BC3C-FD45A9449F03-low.png)

5. A new entry should be created which will have the highest
mode number.

- You can update the
  attributes of the new or existing modes by clicking in their respective boxes
  and change them.
- Click Save to confirm the
  update.

![](GUID-C1ED9133-7AD0-4DA9-BF8B-CECF24AC7061-low.png)

6. To add a new operator, click on Operators > Add
Operator.

![](GUID-90B38E59-0498-43C9-AA0D-DC4BB8493048-low.png)

7. You can repeat step #6 to add/update the new operator
and its attributes.

8. To add a new vehicle type, click on Vehicle Types >
Add Vehicle Types.

![](GUID-68480EF3-2F73-41A5-B110-37AD1F1C64AC-low.png)

9. You can repeat step #6 to add/update the new vehicle
type and its attributes.
